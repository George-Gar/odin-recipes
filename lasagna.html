How to make Lasagna!

this is lasagna above, its pretty good. sometimes i like it but most of the time i dont like it.

Pasta tastes good but it makes you unproductive because of how heavy it is.

ingredients

  • water
  • pasta
  • red sauce
  • ground turkey
  • olive oil
  • cheese

steps for cooking

  1. boil water
  2. cook ground turkey
  3. add olive oil to water then add pasta
  4. layer ground turkey and pasta together
  5. layer cheese throughout as well
  6. bake
  7. eat
